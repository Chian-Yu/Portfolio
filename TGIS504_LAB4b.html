App Introduction

Buy groceries but do not want to go out? Grocery Store Delivery Service Survey can help!

During the pandemic, our shopping habits changed. Almost every grocery store provides a delivery service, but not every grocery store provides the goods we need. The data in Grocery Store Delivery Service Survey contains the grocery's name, address, phone, delivery service, comments, product categories, open day, and location. Cannot find the grocery store on the map? You can also add by yourself! With it, you can find the grocery store you want.

HOW TO ADD DATA

  1. Click the marker button, add the grocery store on the map.
  2. Fill in the form.
    *Click the pen button to edit and the garbage can button to delete.
  3. Submit the form.
    * You cannot re-edit after you submit the data.
