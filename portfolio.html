TGIS503_Lab3: Earthquakes Occur in Past 30 Days

This website presents two types of earthquakes interactive map and data come from the USGS Live Earthquake Feed and is updated every minute. The first map uses a time slider to show the location of the earthquake in each of the past 30 days. The second map uses the heat map to show the frequency of the earthquake in the past 30 days.
